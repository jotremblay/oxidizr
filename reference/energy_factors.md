# Energy conversion factors

Default energy conversion factors (kcal/g) for substrate oxidation

## Usage

``` r
energy_factors
```

## Format

A named list with three elements:

- cho:

  Carbohydrate energy factor (default: 3.72 kcal/g)

- fat:

  Fat energy factor (default: 9.44 kcal/g)

- protein:

  Protein energy factor (default: 4.70 kcal/g)
